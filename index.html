Decription Of Me

I am a highly creative and versatile graphic and web designer with 4 years of experience. I have a multitude of experience in the field of branding, print media & user interface design. I have worked with companies across Ontario and would like to expand further. Currently, I am based in London, Canada. In my work I try to transform highly complex concepts into simple, elegant and thoughtful designs which are easy to read and visually balanced. In my opinion the most important thing is to create designs which have a "WOW" factor in them. I am passionate about what I do and committed to delivering high calibre design, consistent communication and follow-through every time.
